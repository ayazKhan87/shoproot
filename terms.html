Introduction

ShopRoot is a platform that connects users with shops around them. Users can submit product inquiries, and shops can respond to those inquiries. The app also allows shops to register and provide their details, including their products and services.

User Registration

To use ShopRoot, users must provide accurate registration information including full name, email address, contact number, and location. By submitting this information, you agree to receive updates and notifications from ShopRoot.

Shop Registration

Shops must provide accurate business information including shop name, image, location, and owner's name. Registration is currently free, with premium features planned for future introduction.
